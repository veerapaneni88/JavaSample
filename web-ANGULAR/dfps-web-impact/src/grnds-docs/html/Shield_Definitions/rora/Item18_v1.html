Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

18. Other person has access to the client's finances.


  • No: No others have access to the client's finances.
  • Yes: Others have access to the client's finances/assets. Examples include the following:


  • Another person is listed on the client's financial accounts;


  • Another person is able to access the client's finances without the client's knowledge;


  • Another person has power of attorney for financial matters on behalf of the client.


If "b" is marked, indicate whether the person with access is the caretaker, the alleged perpetrator, a family member, or other person.
