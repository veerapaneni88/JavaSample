Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

7. Prior APS investigation during which client was identified as lacking capacity or exhibited diminished cognitive functioning.


  • No: No cognitive functioning issues previously identified.
  • Yes: The client was identified as lacking capacity or having diminished cognitive functioning as part of a prior investigation.

The client lacks one or more basic mental skills (e.g., orientation; thought process; recall of events; understanding, identifying, and solving problems) to perform everyday activities and make decisions that affect his/her health, safety, and well-being. Diminished cognitive functioning may be identified through diagnosis or observation.
