Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

4. Prior APS investigation resulted in client receiving a medical diagnosis/treatment for a physical condition.


  • No: No medical diagnosis or treatment for a physical health condition (as opposed to mental) resulted from a prior APS investigation.
  • Yes: The client received medical diagnosis or treatment (e.g., medical evaluation, prescriptions, medical or adaptive equipment, home health services, or hospitalization) for a physical health condition as a result of prior APS investigation and intervention.

The following point values are used to calculate the risk score.
