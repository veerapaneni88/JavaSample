Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

12. Client previously refused services.


  • No: The client has never needed, refused, or withdrawn from mental health or APS services.
  • Yes, client has refused mental health services: The client was referred for mental health services but refused or withdrew.
  • Yes, client has refused APS services, excluding mental health services: The client refused or withdrew from APS services, excluding mental health, in a previous APS investigation.

The following point values are used to calculate the risk score.
