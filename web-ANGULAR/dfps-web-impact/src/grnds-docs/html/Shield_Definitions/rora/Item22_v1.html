Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

22. Evidence of client's physical limitations.


  • No: No evidence of physical limitations.
  • Yes: Evidence of client's physical limitations


    • The client demonstrates physical limitations that affect performance of specific physical tasks yet experiences no significant interference with his/her daily life. While not a disability, limitations may include but are not limited to abilities such as those required for walking, grasping, and climbing stairs.
