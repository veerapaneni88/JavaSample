Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

2. Prior APS investigations for self-neglect allegation.


  • None: No prior investigations involved allegations of self-neglect.
  • One or two: The client was the subject of one or two APS investigation included allegations of self-neglect, regardless of validation decision prior to the current investigation.
  • Three or more: The client was the subject of three or more APS investigation included allegations of self-neglect, regardless of validation decision prior to the current investigation.

The following point values are used to calculate the risk score.
