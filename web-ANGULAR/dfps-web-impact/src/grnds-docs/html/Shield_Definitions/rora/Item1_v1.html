Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

1. Number of prior APS investigations of any type.


  • None: No APS investigations occurred prior to the current investigation.
  • One: The client was the subject of one APS investigation prior to the current investigation.
  • Two or more: The client was the subject of two or more APS investigations prior to the current investigation.
