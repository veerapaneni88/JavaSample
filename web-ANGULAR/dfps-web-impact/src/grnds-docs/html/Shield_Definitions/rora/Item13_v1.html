Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

13. Client previously abused, neglected, or exploited another person.


  • No: There is no indication that the client has perpetrated abuse, neglect, or exploitation against another person as an adult.
  • Yes: The client perpetrated abuse, neglect, or exploitation on a child and/or other adult. Exclude incidents of aggression/combativeness that result from dementia, brain injury, or other cognitive impairment. Include credible reports of abuse/neglect /exploitation that were not reported to APS or CPS, law enforcement, etc.

Include credible reports of domestic violence. Domestic violence can involve one or more physical assaults and/or periods of intimidation/threats/harassment.
