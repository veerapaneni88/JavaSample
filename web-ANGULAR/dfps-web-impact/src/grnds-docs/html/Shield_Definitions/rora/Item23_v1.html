Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

23. Client has a current or prior diagnosed or suspected mental health concern.


  • No: The client has never been diagnosed or has never demonstrated or displayed symptoms of a mental health concern.
  • Yes: The client has been diagnosed with a mental health condition or has demonstrated or displayed symptoms of mental health concern (current or past).
