Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

11. Home health services or provider services received prior to the current intake.


  • No: No prior receipt of provider or home health services.
  • Yes: Prior to this current intake, the client has received provider or home health services including but not limited to: community attendant services, skilled care, primary home care services, or state-funded or private pay services.
