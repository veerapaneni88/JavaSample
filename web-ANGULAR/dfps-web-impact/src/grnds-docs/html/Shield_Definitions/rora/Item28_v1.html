Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

28. Client has or has had an alcohol/drug abuse problem.


  • No: The client does not have a current or past alcohol or drug abuse problem.
  • Yes: The client has/had current alcohol or drug abuse.

  • Alcohol: The client has a past or present alcohol abuse problem that interferes or interfered with daily functioning.


  • Drugs: The client has a past or present drug abuse problem that interferes or interfered with daily functioning (include prescription and/or over-the-counter drug or other drug misuse).

The following point values are used to calculate the risk score.
