Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

21. Client with capacity to consent currently refuses services.


  • No: No services are required, the client has accepted services offered as a result of the current investigation, or the client has been assessed and found to lack capacity.
  • Yes: The client, who has the capacity to consent, is capable of accepting and/or accessing needed resources or services but chooses not to do so. Include refusal to accept community-based services (e.g., Meals on Wheels, provider services, home health services).
