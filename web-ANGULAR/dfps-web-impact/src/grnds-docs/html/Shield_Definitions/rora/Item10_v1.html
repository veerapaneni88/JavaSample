Risk of Recidivism Assessment Definition

10. Client displayed inappropriate affect or mood or extreme emotional behaviors as part of a prior investigation or case.


  • No: The client did not demonstrate inappropriate affect or mood or extreme emotional behaviors during a prior investigation.
  • Yes: The client displayed inappropriate affect or mood or extreme emotional behaviors during a prior investigation. Examples of behaviors include but are not limited to: inappropriate mood, angry outbursts, tearfulness. These behaviors may have resulted in consistently firing aides, aides consistently quitting, or client's inability to maintain supportive relationships.
