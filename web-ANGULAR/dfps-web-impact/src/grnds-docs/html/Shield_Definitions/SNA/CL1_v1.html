Strengths and Needs Assessment Definition

CL1. Physical Health


  1. No concerns related to physical health.
    • Client receives preventive and/or regular health care, i.e., client sees a medical professional on a regular or as needed basis.
    • Client is compliant with medication regimen.
    • Client may have a physical condition (e.g., poor muscular functioning, skin condition, high blood pressure, diabetes) or sensory disability (e.g., hearing or visually impaired), but the condition is well-managed and there are no concerns related to physical health.

  2. Some concerns related to physical health.
    Client has moderate health problems that require medical attention AND:
    • Has no primary care physician; OR
    • Does not see a medical professional as needed; OR
    • Does not follow prescribed treatment, including medication regimen.

  3. Significant concerns related to physical health.
    Client has severe health problems AND:
    • Is not receiving necessary medical care; OR
    • Is not receiving medication; OR
    • Does not follow prescribed treatment.
