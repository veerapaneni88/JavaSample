Strengths and Needs Assessment Definition

CL8. Resource Management


  1. Financial resources are adequately managed.
    Client is able or relies on others to manage finances without any problems or discord.

  2. Financial resources are not well managed.
    • The client or another person who manages the client's financial resources is not able or willing to manage resources that would otherwise be sufficient for necessities.
    • Poor resource management results in occasional problems with meeting adequate necessities (e.g., shelter, food, medications, utilities, and clothing).

  3. Financial resources are severely mismanaged.
    • Client is unable or unwilling to provide for necessities to the extent that shelter, food, clothing, medications, and/or utilities are inadequate.
    • Client is completely dependent on others for managing his/her financial resources and that management is severely inadequate.
