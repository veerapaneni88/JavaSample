Strengths and Needs Assessment Definition

CL10. Social/Community Support System


Consider the client's support from religious, cultural, or community organizations; commercial or contractual relationships; and family/friends.

  1. Adequate support system.
    • As needs arise, client uses family; friends; or cultural, religious, and community resources to provide support and/or services such as transportation, emotional support, help with minor household repairs, etc.
    • Client is willing to ask for assistance when needed.

  2. Limited support system.
    • Support system does not consistently meet client's needs.
    • Client's support is irregular in quantity and/or frequency.
    • Client has limited knowledge of available resources such as transportation, emotional support, help with minor household repairs, etc.

  3. No support system.
    • Client is physically or socially isolated to the extent that health and safety are affected.
    • Client has no knowledge of formal support systems.
    • Client does not use extended family, friends, and community resources for help with transportation, emotional support, household repairs, etc.
    • Client has exhausted available support systems.
