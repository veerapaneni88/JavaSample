Strengths and Needs Assessment Definition

CL7. Financial Resources


  1. Financial resources, including benefits, are sufficient to meet basic needs.
    Client has access to finances and/or benefits that are sufficient to provide for all necessities (e.g., shelter, food, clothing, medications, utilities).

  2. Financial resources, including benefits, are insufficient.
    • Available financial resources and/or benefits barely provide for necessities or services.
    • Client is financially semi-dependent on others.

  3. Financial resources, including benefits, are nonexistent or severely limited.
    Client does not have sufficient financial resources and/or benefits to meet basic needs.
