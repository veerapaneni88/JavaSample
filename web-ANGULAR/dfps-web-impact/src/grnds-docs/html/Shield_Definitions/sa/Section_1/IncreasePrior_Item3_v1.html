Safety Assessment Definition

Need for protection/relocation from a dangerous or violent environment.


The living conditions are believed to be dangerous AND are likely to result in imminent harm or illness OR the alleged victim requires immediate relocation in order to avoid imminent harm. Examples include but are not limited to: the alleged perpetrator remains in the home and there are no protective services in place, or there is a history of violence or neglect to the extent that the alleged victim has experienced physical injury or illness in the past AND the alleged victim is currently in imminent danger of harm.
