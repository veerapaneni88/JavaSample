Safety Assessment Definition

Is referral to another investigatory agency (including law enforcement) required?


Mark "yes" if any of the following conditions are met:

  • The complaint involves policies or practices conducted within a certified and licensed facility;
  • The complaint involves waste, abuse, or fraud within the Texas Health and Human Services system;
  • The complaint involves conduct or practices of a licensed person and does not allege abuse, neglect, or exploitation;
  • The complaint involves a case with a guardian appointed by the court or a pending guardianship; or
  • The caseworker suspects allegations of abuse, neglect, or exploitation that constitute a criminal offense.
